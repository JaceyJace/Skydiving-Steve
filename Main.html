Sky Diving Steve

Welcome all to Skydiving Steve! Steve wants to jump from a plane but needs your help to navigate him left or right
to avoid any obstacles that he might encounter.
On your way down you'll see clouds, which you should avoid, and birds which you should also avoid!
You'll also come across shinny gold coins, don't avoid those, pick them up! Be sure to avoid those nasty branches closer to the bottom!		 About Skydiving Steve
